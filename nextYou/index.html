Core Product Capabilities

  • Challenge management: start, edit, extend, complete, and preserve history.
  • Task system: configure units, targets, icons, details, and schedule slots.
  • Progress intelligence: daily stats, completion rates, streaks, and task-level summaries.
  • AI coaching flow: profile setup and generated weekly/final reports.
  • Subscription support: StoreKit premium features, restore purchases, entitlement sync.
  • Data sync architecture: local-first with optional iCloud/CloudKit sync.
  • Authentication: Sign in with Apple and secure Keychain handling.

Privacy & Data Footprint
